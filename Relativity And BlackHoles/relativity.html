Relativity Of Simultaneity

  • Einstein and Michele are sitting in the middle of train and platform respectively. When simulate is clicked, both pass eachother and lightning strikes on both ends of train.
  • Velocity of objects in motion = 0.3c
  • Click simulate to see from Einstein and Michele's perspective
  • Conclusion: Events that are simultaneous for one observer are not simultaneous to another observer who is at contsant motion relative to the first.
  • Based on this and the postulate that the speed of light is constant in either direction, purple lightning indeed occurred before the red.
